Our Heritage

Situated in the historic Sharada Industrial Estate, we represent the best of Northern Nigeria's legendary leather tradition. Our facility employs over 150 skilled artisans and modern technicians who bring decades of combined expertise to every product. We source the finest raw materials from across Nigeria and process them using cutting-edge tanning and finishing technologies. Our commitment to quality has made us a preferred supplier to major Nigerian retailers, exporters, and government institutions. We're not just manufacturing leather goods; we're preserving and elevating Nigeria's rich leather heritage while creating sustainable employment for our community.

Manufacturing Excellence

Our 5,000 square meter facility houses advanced tanning drums, finishing equipment, and quality control laboratories that ensure every batch meets stringent standards. We employ eco-friendly tanning processes that minimize environmental impact while maximizing leather quality and durability.

Our production line handles everything from raw hide procurement through final product delivery, with rigorous quality checks at every stage. We maintain ISO-compliant processes and can scale production from small artisan batches to large industrial orders. Our technical team works closely with clients to develop custom leather specifications, colors, and finishes that bring your vision to life while maintaining our trademark Sharada quality.

Sustainability & Community

At Sharada Leather Industries, we're committed to responsible manufacturing that benefits both business and community. Our waste management systems convert leather scraps into valuable by-products, while our water treatment facilities ensure environmental compliance. We actively train young Nigerians in leather craftsmanship, preserving traditional skills while introducing modern techniques. Our local sourcing programs support Nigerian farmers and hide collectors, creating a sustainable value chain that strengthens the entire industry. We partner with technical institutions in Kano to develop curriculum that produces industry-ready graduates. By choosing Sharada, you support a business that's investing in Nigeria's industrial future, creating quality jobs, and manufacturing with conscience.
